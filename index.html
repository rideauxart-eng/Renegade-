001 — VISION

THE STUDIO

A content production studio creating horror-beautiful art at the intersection of manga and classical fine art.

Rain-soaked. Game-native. Built for mass appeal.

Every piece expresses one or more of four core emotions. The most powerful work layers them together—fear bleeding into intrigue, sensuality edged with mystery.

002 — EMOTIONS

THE FOUR PILLARS

FEAR

Wide eyes, parted mouth, harsh lighting. The moment before the worst happens.

SUBTLE → BUILDING → PEAK → FROZEN

INTRIGUE

Narrowed eyes, hint of smile, half-lit. She knows something you don't.

CURIOUS → KNOWING → DANGEROUS → PREDATORY

SENSUALITY

Heavy-lidded, relaxed, warm tones. Power in beauty, allure with depth.

SUBTLE → AWARE → INVITING → DANGEROUS

MYSTERY

Unreadable, distant, obscured. The face that reveals nothing.

VEILED → DISTANT → UNKNOWABLE → ABSENT
